About Hemegene

Hemegene is a trusted knowledge base for gene mutations related to Acute Myeloid Leukemia (AML). Developed through collaboration between medical experts and data scientists, Hemegene offers a user-friendly platform for healthcare professionals to explore essential genetic information crucial for AML diagnosis and treatment.

Our mission is to provide accurate, easily accessible, up-to-date genetic data that supports informed decision-making in precision medicine, ultimately improving outcomes for AML patients. Furthermore, our newly integrated Interactive Nutrition Map empowers researchers to uncover vital regional dietary patterns—such as Iron and Vitamin B12 densities—bridging the gap between localized nutritional awareness and advanced therapeutic interventions.
