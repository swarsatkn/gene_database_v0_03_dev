About Hemegene

Hemegene is a trusted clinical knowledge base for gene mutations related to Acute Myeloid Leukemia (AML). Developed by HemeTeam India and Jivana AI under the aegis of Fortis Memorial Research Institute (FMRI), Hemegene offers a user-friendly platform for healthcare professionals to explore essential genetic information crucial for AML diagnosis and treatment.

Our mission is to provide accurate, easily accessible, and up-to-date genetic data that supports informed decision-making in precision medicine, ultimately improving outcomes for AML patients.

Additionally, the newly integrated Interactive Nutrition Map is a dedicated initiative for spreading awareness about regional nutritional statuses—such as Iron and Vitamin B12 densities—helping users understand localized dietary needs and their impact on health.
