About Us

Hemegene is a trusted knowledge base for gene mutations related to Acute Myeloid Leukemia (AML). Developed through collaboration between medical experts and data scientists, Hemegene offers a user-friendly platform for healthcare professionals to explore essential genetic information crucial for AML diagnosis and treatment.

Our mission is to provide accurate, easily accessible, up-to-date genetic data that supports informed decision-making in precision medicine, ultimately improving outcomes for AML patients.
