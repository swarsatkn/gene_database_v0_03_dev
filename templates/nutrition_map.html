How this map benefits you?

Visualizing geographic nutrition profiles allows medical experts and researchers to quickly correlate regional dietary patterns with conditions such as Acute Myeloid Leukemia (AML) and anemia. By identifying localized deficiencies or an abundance of Iron and Vitamin B12, healthcare providers can confidently prescribe proactive interventions, suggest geographically tailored dietary plans, and improve the precision of patient screening and therapy.
